How will you begin to dream something no machine can dream?



Octavia Butler's "Parable of the Sower" puts words to how I craft my future. I have not finished the book, but the wisdom and bravery of the young protagonist, Lauren, already inspires me. Here are some of Lauren's words:



"God is Power --- Infinite, Irresistible, Inexorable, Indifferent. And yet, God is Pliable --- Trickster, Teacher, Chaos, Clay. God exists to be shaped. God is Change."
